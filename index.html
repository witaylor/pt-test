Please note that this is an early proof of concept. There are some features that are missing, and others may not work as intended.

On the next page, you will be prompted to upload an image. It will then be analysed and your pose will be estimated. Right now, the app won't give you any information on your posture but will mark 'landmarks' on your body, such as your shoulders and hips.

For the best results, make sure that these landmarks are easily visible. Avoid wearing long and/or baggy clothing.

By default, your image will never be stored and will only be visible to you.

You may allow us to save your image for analytical purposes by ticking the checkbox on the next page. In this case, your image will be securely stored, only visible to the sole developer of this app, and will only be used to test the accuracy of the pose estimation model.
